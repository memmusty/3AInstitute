Safety and Assurance in Systems


Introduction

As systems engineer and analyst in the New Branch of Engineering (NBE) - Applied Cybernetics, when exploring systems at scale, just understanding system's behaviors and relationships won't be enough. How do we interrogate complex systems for assurance and safety? In this activity, I used the STAMP STPA to explore potential risky, hazardous, and fatal behaviors an autonomous vehicle may exhibit when deployed on the streets of Lagos, Nigeria.
This page provides an overview of my experience learning and conducting hazard analysis with STAMP STPA, understanding how autonomous vehicles function, coupling it with my experience as Lagos resident. My reflection on the STPA method, its percieved applicability and limitations are documented here

Achievement

At the end of the activities, I learned how to:
  • Perform hazard analysis with STPA
  • Anticipate hazardous situations from different perspectives
